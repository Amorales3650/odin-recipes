Scrambled Eggs

Description:

Eggs mixed in a bowl then cooked on the stove

Ingredients

  • Eggs
  • Seasoning & spices
  • water (optional)

steps

  1. mix eggs in bowl
  2. (optional) add water, only a splash
  3. put olive oil on pan and sprinkle salt, let sit for like 5 min
  4. add eggs to pan
  5. add seasonings while eggs r mostly liquid
  6. stir occasionally after it starts to solididfy usually after like 3-5 minutes
  7. cook to prefeference then serve
