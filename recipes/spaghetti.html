Spaghetti

Description:

Itsa noodles anda pasta sauce witha de meata balla! mama mia!

Ingredients

  • spaghetti noodles
  • pasta sauce
  • meatballs
  • herbs & spices

steps

  1. make sure oven is at ~400 degrees farenheit put the meatballs in the oven
  2. boil noodles for like 10 min/ when they get soft
  3. after like 5 min dump the pasta sauce into another pan and start warmin it up, add spices to the sauce
  4. this is optional but add vegetables/cooked meat and or veggie ground beef grounds to sauce (muy bueno)
  5. when noodles r done check that meatballs r cooked then put in pasta pot
  6. wallah ur done add them together
